About

This is Mishelle Werner, she is a mother of two sweet boys and who enjoys, in her free time, creative writing, baking cakes and creating fun arts and crafts. Mishelle loves to bake layered cakes in her free time to suit different event that happen in her large family.

Whether it’s a birthday cake or just an out of the blue whim, Mishelle can make some tasty cakes. On the other end of the spectrum, whenever time allows, Mishelle is writing or specifically typing out one of her fantasy writing stories she is working hard to finish. She hopes to eventually go through the pain staking process of trying to publish one of her novels, in fact, it is one of her major life goals that she is extremely adamant about. She runs her own craft business selling various forms of different soap and candles spanning a large array of different scents, shapes, and sizes. Those crafts also include home goods using her Cricut machine which allows creation of other various craft products from car decals/ bumper stickers, mug designs, iron on shirt designs and more.

All this while working a full-time job, being a loving and caring mother for two boys and taking online classes from a local university. Coming up on her final years in university, Mishelle is going to be graduating with a bachelor’s degree in Human Resources and is looking forward to eventually working in the human resources field for a top company in the not-so-distant future.
